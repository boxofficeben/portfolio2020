About Benjamin

Benjamin is a web content administrator at a non-profit organization with over two years experience in web development and design. He currently completed a web design certification from the New York State certified school Noble Desktop. Previously, he completed certifications in front-end web development at General Assembly (2018-2019) and at NYU’s Professional Studies in web development and design (2018). Recently, Benjamin was promoted from executive assistant and web associate to web content administrator after pursuing his passion in maintaining and designing his organization’s website.

Benjamin is passionate about mission driven organization and personal websites that fuel the passions of others. His work with World Capital Confidential, was his first professional job and continues to provide site maintenance and updates. Benjamin is an avid theater, food and travel enthusiast with a passion in coffee and wine. On the side, Benjamin loves to bake and host dinner parties. In the future, Benjamin would like to have his own web development freelance job where he could spend three months out of the year in France.
